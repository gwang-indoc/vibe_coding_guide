# Claude Code: Context "Dumb Zone" Thresholds

Observed degradation kicks in around ~40% context utilization — "you hit this point where you have degrading results."

## Guidance by Experience Level

**Newcomers:** "Shoot to keep it under 40%, and if you get up to 60%, think about wrapping it up."

**Experienced users:** "Aggressively keep it below 30%" — push to 60% only on simple tasks.

## Reset Strategies

Use `/compact` or `/clear` to reset when switching tasks.
